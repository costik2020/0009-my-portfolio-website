About Me

I am Constantin, since I know myself I did enjoy playing with computers, coding and programming, interested in Tech and Science. I started to self taught and self study programming since 5 years ago in my off days from my full-time job. I started with CS50 which was an online computer science course from Harvard. And after that course I went into reading and learning from countless number of places from all over the Internet. To enumerate a few places: codecademy, MDN Documentation, stackoverflow, freeCodeCamp, CSS-Tricks, W3schools, and the rest is history...

As my work experience I worked in many fields like: construction, wall painter, furniture removals, security, car park steward, traffic control staff, janitor, team supervisor, scissor lift operator, guest service assistant. But the most I worked for over 7 years at London Designer Outlet (LDO) shopping center in Wembley, London, UK. This place is located between Wembley Stadium and SSE Arena. I worked at LDO since 2014 to December 2021. Because in December I resigned from my job for ABM Company for the reason of having more time to self study coding and Web Development.

When I don't work or I don't study new things I am passioned about video games and I love cycling and riding my Motorcycle.
